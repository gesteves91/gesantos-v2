Biography

Software Developer, Gamer, Soccer and Rock n Roll addicted.

Technology has been in my veins since I was a kid. I remember being amazed by computers and video games very early in my life. As a teenager, I had no doubts that I wanted to study technology when I reached college. Then my dream came true in 2011, when I started my first technical degree in informatics. Even now I am still amazed by it.

My main goal is to get my PhD in computer science either in the USA or in Brazil. I see myself in the middle of two different paths, one professional and the other educational. Being a software developer and a teaching assistant for so long makes the decision of being either a Professor or an Engineer very hard. I feel like my professional experience can help me to become a better professor because I have developed not only theoretical skills but also technical skills. In the same way, my teaching experience helps me to contribute to my team more effectively.

Academic Experience

Bachelor of Information Systems at Pontifícia Universidade Católica de Minas Gerais (Brazil) and Indiana University (USA)

Back in 2011, I started a technical degree in Informatics. Even before my graduation, I had already started my undergraduate degree at PUC Minas in Brazil. I had the opportunity to do part of my degree as an exchange student at IU in the USA. I have developed a strong interest in research during college, and have published articles in different computer science areas such as human-computer interaction (semiotic engineering), databases (sentiment analysis) and games. I was a teaching assistant at PUC Minas for 2 years helping students taking computer programming-related courses.
