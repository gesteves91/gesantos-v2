Biography

Software Developer, Gamer, Soccer and Rock n Roll addicted.

Technology is in my vein since I was a little kid. I remember being amazed by computers and videogames very early in my life. In my teenager years, I have no doubts that I would like to study technology when I reach College. Then, my dream came true in 2011, when I started my first technical degree in Informatics. I am still amazed by it.

My main goal is to get my PhD in Computer Science either in the USA or in Brazil. I consider myself being at the very middle of the two different paths, professional and educational. Being a Software Developer and a Teacher Assistant for so long makes me feel stuck in the dilemma of being either a Professor or an Engineer when I get my PhD. Either way, I know I will do my best in both areas.
